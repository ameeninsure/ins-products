# Health Insurance

Insurance that covers medical expenses, hospitalization, and healthcare services in Oman.

## Individual Health Insurance

Personal medical coverage for individuals and families.

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Marital Status
- Number of Dependents
- Medical History Questionnaire
- ID Copy (Image)
- Passport Photo (Image)

## Group Health Insurance

Medical coverage for employees and their families.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Number of Employees
- Employee List (Excel File)
- Employee ID Copies (Images)
- Company Trade License (Image)
- Authorization Letter (Image)
