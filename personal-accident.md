# Personal Accident Insurance

Insurance that pays financial compensation to the individual or their family in case of accidents resulting in injury, disability, or death.

## Individual Accident Insurance

Personal accident coverage for individuals.

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Occupation
- Coverage Amount
- Beneficiary Name
- Beneficiary Relationship
- ID Copy (Image)

## Group Accident Insurance

Accident coverage for employees or group members.

**Required Information:**
- Company/Organization Name
- Company Registration Number
- Number of Members
- Member List (Excel File)
- Member ID Copies (Images)
- Coverage Amount Per Member
- Company Trade License (Image)
- Authorization Letter (Image)
