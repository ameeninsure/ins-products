# Property Insurance

Insurance that protects commercial and industrial properties against fire, earthquakes, floods, and other unexpected incidents.

## Commercial Property Insurance

Coverage for business properties and offices.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Property Address
- Property Type
- Building Area (sq meters)
- Property Value
- Business Type
- Property Ownership Document (Image)
- Property Photos (Multiple Images)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)

## Industrial Property Insurance

Coverage for factories and industrial facilities.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Facility Address
- Facility Type
- Building Area (sq meters)
- Equipment Value
- Total Property Value
- Industry Type
- Safety Certificates (Image)
- Property Ownership Document (Image)
- Facility Photos (Multiple Images)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
