# Liability Insurance

Insurance that covers legal liability of individuals and businesses for damages caused to third parties.

## General Liability Insurance

Coverage for bodily injury and property damage liability.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Business Type
- Business Activity
- Number of Employees
- Annual Revenue
- Coverage Amount Required
- Business Location
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)

## Product Liability Insurance

Coverage for liability from manufactured or sold products.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Products List
- Annual Sales Volume
- Distribution Channels
- Export Markets
- Coverage Amount Required
- Product Certificates (Images)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
