# Motor Insurance

Insurance that provides coverage for vehicle damages, third-party liability, and road accidents in Oman.

## Third Party Liability Insurance

Coverage for damages caused to others (people and property).

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- License Number
- Phone Number
- Plate Number
- Plate Character (English)
- Plate Character (Arabic)
- Driving License (Image)
- Vehicle Registration (Image)

## Comprehensive Insurance

Full coverage including own vehicle damages, theft, and third-party liability.

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- License Number
- Phone Number
- Plate Number
- Plate Character (English)
- Plate Character (Arabic)
- Vehicle Make and Model
- Vehicle Year
- Vehicle Value
- Driving License (Image)
- Vehicle Registration (Image)
- Vehicle Photos (Multiple Images)
