# Domestic Helper Insurance

Insurance that covers employer's liability towards domestic workers and helpers against accidents and illnesses.

## Domestic Worker Comprehensive Insurance

Full coverage including medical and work injury.

**Required Information:**
- Employer Name
- Employer ID Number
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Home Address
- Worker Name
- Worker Passport Number
- Worker Nationality
- Worker Date of Birth
- Contract Start Date
- Employer ID Copy (Image)
- Worker Passport Copy (Image)
- Worker Photo (Image)
- Employment Contract (Image)
