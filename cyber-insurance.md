# Cyber Insurance

Insurance that covers businesses against damages from cyber attacks, data breaches, and online crimes.

## Cyber Liability Insurance

Coverage for cyber attacks and digital security incidents.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Business Type
- IT Infrastructure Details
- Number of Employees
- Annual Revenue
- Data Types Handled
- Current Security Measures
- Coverage Amount Required
- IT Security Assessment (Image)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)

## Data Breach Insurance

Coverage for customer data breach incidents.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Business Type
- Number of Customer Records
- Data Storage Methods
- Annual Revenue
- Previous Incidents History
- Data Protection Policies
- Coverage Amount Required
- Data Security Certificates (Images)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
