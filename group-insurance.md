# Group Insurance

Insurance that provides collective health or life coverage for employees of an organization at more favorable rates.

## Group Health Insurance

Collective medical coverage for employees.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Number of Employees
- Employee List with Details (Excel File)
- Coverage Start Date
- Employee ID Copies (Images)
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
- Authorization Letter (Image)

## Group Life Insurance

Collective life coverage for employees.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name

- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Number of Employees
- Employee List with Details (Excel File)
- Coverage Amount Per Employee
