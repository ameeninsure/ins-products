# Life Insurance

Insurance that guarantees financial support for family and dependents in case of death or permanent disability of the insured.

## Term Life Insurance

Coverage for a specific period.

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Occupation
- Monthly Income
- Coverage Amount
- Policy Term (Years)
- Beneficiary Name
- Beneficiary Relationship
- Medical History Questionnaire
- ID Copy (Image)
- Income Proof (Image)

## Whole Life Insurance

Lifetime coverage with savings component.

**Required Information:**
- Customer Name
- ID Number
- Date of Birth
- Gender
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Occupation
- Monthly Income
- Coverage Amount
- Beneficiary Name
- Beneficiary Relationship
- Medical History Questionnaire
- Medical Report (Image)
- ID Copy (Image)
- Income Proof (Image)
