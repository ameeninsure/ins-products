# Workmen Compensation Insurance

Insurance that covers medical expenses and compensation for workers in case of workplace accidents or occupational diseases.

## Mandatory Workmen Compensation

Legally required coverage for all employees.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Number of Workers
- Worker List with Salaries (Excel File)
- Industry Type
- Business Activity
- Total Annual Wages
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
- Employee Labor Cards (Images)

## Extended Workmen Compensation

Additional voluntary coverage beyond legal requirements.

**Required Information:**
- Company Name
- Company Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Number of Workers
- Worker List with Salaries (Excel File)
- Desired Coverage Extensions
- Industry Type
- Business Activity
- Total Annual Wages
- Trade License (Image)
- Company Registration (Image)
- Employee Labor Cards (Images)
- Safety Records (Image)
