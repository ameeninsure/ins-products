# Professional Indemnity Insurance

Insurance that covers professionals and consultants against claims arising from professional errors or negligence.

## Professional Indemnity

Coverage for consultants and professional service providers.

**Required Information:**
- Professional/Company Name
- Registration Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Profession Type
- Years of Experience
- Number of Staff
- Annual Revenue
- Coverage Amount Required
- Professional License (Image)
- Qualifications Certificates (Images)
- Company Registration (Image)

## Medical Malpractice Insurance

Coverage for medical professionals and facilities.

**Required Information:**
- Doctor/Facility Name
- Medical License Number
- Contact Person Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Specialization
- Years of Practice
- Number of Medical Staff
- Annual Patient Volume
- Coverage Amount Required
- Medical License (Image)
- Facility License (Image)
- Medical Qualifications (Images)
- Registration Certificate (Image)
